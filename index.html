Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a real cryptocurrency or just points? +

NEURONS are real cryptocurrency tokens that can be traded on exchanges and have monetary value. They're built as ERC-20 tokens on blockchain networks.

How do I know my learning is being tracked fairly? +

All proofs are cryptographically signed using EIP-712 standards and verified on blockchain, making fraud nearly impossible. The verification process is transparent and immutable.

What if I don't understand blockchain technology? +

You don't need to! The platform handles all the technical details. Just connect a crypto wallet (like MetaMask), learn, and earn. The blockchain works behind the scenes.

Are there fees for receiving tokens? +

The platform covers gas fees for reward distribution. You only pay network fees when transferring tokens yourself to other wallets or exchanges.

Can I use this on mobile? +

Yes! The platform works on mobile browsers with supported crypto wallets. You can learn and earn on the go.
